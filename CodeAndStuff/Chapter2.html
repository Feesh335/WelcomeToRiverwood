Nera'Vei Ruins

Overview

After heading over from Oldtown, the players find the town is in chaos and get a message from the Ordo Novis to investiage Meridan's last know whereabouts in the Nere'Vei ruins. Exploring the ruins reveals that they have mostly been explored, and there is a large, ominous looking central chamber with an empty pedestal. Entering the room triggers arcane traps placed by Maureen the Magnificent who is more interested in bragging and showing off than actually trying to fight. Once dealth with, an apprentice mage can be freed from stasis and will explain that Meridan found some sort of relic of unspeakable power a few minutes ago (two months ago, he was in stasis the whole time and unaware of this) and brought it back to Meridan's point. The Ordo Novis sends an envoy with loot to reward the players for this information and urge them to go to Meridan's point to investigate further.

Details

Sidequest: Fallout: New Vellis

  • The Mundus Faithful have set up safe zones around Vellis for people to take shelter from the arcane fallout. They need assistance with cleansing those suffering from arcane sickness and hunting down those who refuse the treatment.

Sidequest: The Delver's Dilemna

  • Archibald the Artificer is found waiting outside the Nere'Vei ruins. He is a historian studying the history of the Mollinstead region and heard rumors that Meridan's expedition into these ruins uncovered many interesting artifacts. He wants a group to follow in so that he can safely examine the ruins.

Encounters

  • Many of the residents of Vellis have become consumed by arcane sickness and gone mad. Arcane tainted wildlife is also fairly common surrounding the city.
  • Maureen the Magnificent and his followers are hiding in the antechamber of the ruins, and the bound tomb guardian guards over the hidden treasure chamber.

Points of Interest

  • Mundus Faithful safe zones, the Nere'Vei ruins, and wanderers may sell goods.
