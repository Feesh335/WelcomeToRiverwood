Anilor

The inseperable twin brother of Corlith, Anilor inherited the divine blood of both parents, granting him the full strength of the divine spark of magic. The divine blood caused him to hear the voices of his ancestors and receive visions of impending disasters. One day he received a vision of a series of disasters stemming from the Mollinstead region and began gathering up a group of adventurers who became one of the first to arrive when the Ordo Novis sent out their plea for help. Anilor and his party helped to evacuate many of the survivors in Vellis before continuing north through the Chittering Dark. While in the Chittering Dark, he began to have nightmares of a black seed falling from the sky and heard a voice telling him to stamp out the corruption before it could spread. Unbeknownst to Anilor, this was not the divine spark, but the lies of Krimerus, the prince of the realm of Nightmares tricking him into an ambush. After fighting through the caverns alongside Corlith, Anilor found that the seed from his visions was actually a meteorite with the essence of Krimerus within it and the two were consumed by his influence, becoming his avatar within Mollinstead. With his body and mind hollowed out, he has taken on a new identity as the prince of worms.

Stats

Race: Human(?)

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Armor Class: 14 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 200

Speed: 30 feet

STR: 11 (+0) | DEX: 12 (+1) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 10 (+0) | WIS: 16 (+3) | CHA: 22 (+6)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Dex +1, Con +6, Wis +3, Cha +11

Damage Immunities: Physical

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: None

Challenge: 21 (33,000 XP)

Passive

Soul of the Worm

Anilor is immune to physical attacks. Splits health with Corlith at the beginning of each round.

Return to Worms

When killed, Anilor becomes a swarm of worms that will reform one day later unless destroyed.

Defiled Divine Blood

When healing, Anilor can reroll any of the dice determining the amount healed once per turn.

Actions

Innate Spellcasting: Anilor's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 16, +9 to hit with spells). He can cast the following spells: At will: Mind Sliverx3 (3d6), Minor Illusion 3/day: Dominate Monster, Regenerate 1/day: Circle of Death

Plague of Worms (Recharge 6). Each creature other than Corlith within 10 feet of Anilor must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure the target takes 22 (5d8) necrotic damage and is blinded and restrained by masses of swarming worms. The affected creature takes 22 (5d8) necrotic damage at the start of each of the larva mage's turns. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Legendary Actions

Cantrip (Costs 1 Action). Anilor casts one cantrip. Compel (Costs 2 Actions). Commands Corlith to take an extra action. Feed (Costs 3 Actions). Each creature restrained by Anilor's Plague of Worms takes 13 (3d8) necrotic damage, and Anilor gains that many hit points.
