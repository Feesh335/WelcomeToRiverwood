Nod

Twisted, cryptic, and pure fucking evil. His haunting gnomish laughter keeps even the most hardened of veterans awake at night in fear. Seems to have knowledge of the timeways that he uses to change the future and never for the better.

Stats

Race: Rock Gnome

Alignment: Chaotic Evil

Armor Class: 12 (Cloth Armor, pre-casts mage armor as the gates open)

Hit Points: 38

Speed: 25 ft

STR: 8 (-1) | DEX: 12 (+1) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 12 (+1) | WIS: 14 (+2) | CHA: 16 (+3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Con + 3, Cha +3

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: Force

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: None

Senses: darkvision 60ft

Languages: Common, elvish, orc

Challenge: 6 (Sorceror (Clockwork Soul))

Passive

Fade Away: Immediately after you take damage, you can use a reaction to magically become invisible until the end of your next turn or until you attack, deal damage, or force someone to make a saving throw. You also teleport somewhere within 30 feet of your choosing. Once you use this ability, you canâ€™t do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

"Spellbook": Nod may "accidentally" drop his spellbook on being hit. The spellbook contains a glyph of warding that will trigger a stinking cloud (http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:stinking-cloud) when opened. The only contents of the book are a drawing of Nod's ass.

Unstable Timepiece: Nod carries an unstable timepiece with him. Upon being hit for over half of his health or with an otherwise fatal blow, the timepiece sucks him into a time portal and spits him out somewhere within 60 feet next turn with the health he had before being hit. The timepiece breaks after use.

List of spells: 4 level 1 slots, 3 level 2 slots, 3 level 3 slots, 3 restore balance, 6 sorcery points.

Diabolical Chronomancer: Nod does not need to use concentration when casting time related spells

Metamagic: Twinned spell and double range spell

Spellcasting: +6 to hit with spells, save DC: 14.

Actions

Dagger: melee weapon attack, +4 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: (1d4 + 1) slashing damage.

Cantrips: Chill touch, fire bolt, mage hand, message, minor illusion

Level one spells: mage armor, thunderwave, alarm, protection from good and evil

Level two spells: blur, invisibility, mirror image, find traps, heat metal

Level three spells: counterspell, glyph of warding, fireball, haste

Restore balance (3 uses): When a creature you can see within 60 feet of you is about to roll a d20 with advantage or disadvantage, you can use your reaction to prevent the roll from being affected by advantage and disadvantage.

Bulwark of law: Starting at 6th level, you can imbue a creature with a shimmering shield of order. As an action, you can expend 1 to 5 sorcery points to create a magical ward around yourself or another creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The ward lasts until you finish a long rest or until you use this feature again. The ward is represented by a number of d8s equal to the number of sorcery points spent to create it. When the warded creature takes damage, it can use its reaction to spend a number of those dice, roll them, and reduce the damage taken by the total of the spent dice.
