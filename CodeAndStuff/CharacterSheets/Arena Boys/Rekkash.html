Rekkash the Broken

Once a mighty and powerful red dragon, Rekkash was defeated by Ogmund the Bold who enslaved him and brought him to the collection. For this feat of strength, Ogmund became a member of the inner circle of Arcellius, while Rekkash became a plaything for combatants of the arena to fight. Rekkash proved to be too powerful for most of the arena teams and so over time, the council saw to it that Rekkash would be broken in. Centuries of torture stripped Rekkash of his personality, leaving behind a broken husk of dulled fury.

Stats

Race: Huge Dragon

Alignment: Chaotic Evil

Armor Class: 18 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 206

Speed: 35 ft, fly 70ft

STR: 23 (+6) | DEX: 8 (-1) | CON: 20 (+5) | INT: 14 (+2) | WIS: 13 (+1) | CHA: 16 (+3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Str + 4, Con + 9, Wis + 3, Cha +7

Damage Immunities: Fire

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: Psychic

Skills: None

Senses: blindsight 30ft, darkvision 120ft, passive Perception 20

Languages: Common, draconic

Challenge: 14 (11,500 XP)

Passive

Legendary Resistance (1/Day). If Rekkash fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Legendary Actions

Rekkash can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Rekkash regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

Detect: Rekkash makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

Tail Attack: Rekkash makes a tail attack.

Wing Attack (costs 2 actions): The dragon beats its wings. Each creature within 10 feet of the dragon must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or take 15 (2d6 + 8) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. The dragon can then fly up to half its flying speed.

Actions

Multiattack: Rekkash makes three attacks: one with his bite and two with his claws. One of the claw attacks always has disadvantage.

Bite: Melee weapon attack, +11 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. Hit: (2d10 + 7) piercing plus (1d6) fire damage.

Claw: Melee weapon attack, +11 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: (2d6 + 6) slashing damage.

Tail: Melee weapon attack, +11 to hit, reach 15 ft, one target. Hit: (2d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6): Fire Breath (Recharge 6). Rekkash exhales fire in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking (14d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Spellcasting: +8 to hit with spells, save DC: 16. 3 casts of fireball/erupting earth
