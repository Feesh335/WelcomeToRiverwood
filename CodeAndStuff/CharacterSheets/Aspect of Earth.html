Aspect of Earth

A set of ritual armor given life through elemental earth magic.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 17 (stone armor)

Hit Points: 125

Speed: 25 ft (hover)

STR: 18 (+4) | DEX: 8 (-1) | CON: 18 (+4) | INT: 9 (-1) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 10 (0)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Non-magical physical

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: Auran, Common

Challenge: 7 (2,900 XP)

Passive

Staggering Weapons

Any landed attacks with the maul causes the target to be unable to use reactions and have disadvantage on dexterity saving throws until the aspect's next turn.

Actions

Multiattack: The aspect of earth makes two maul attacks.

Staggering Maul: Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: 8 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage

Crystalline Armor (Recharge 5). The aspect coats itself in brittle crystals, whenever it takes physical damage, that creature takes 1d8 physical damage. This last two turns.
