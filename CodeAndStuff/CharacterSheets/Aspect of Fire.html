Aspect of Fire

A set of ritual armor given life through elemental fire magic.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 16 (plate armor)

Hit Points: 125

Speed: 30 ft (hover)

STR: 16 (+3) | DEX: 16 (+3) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 9 (-1) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 10 (0)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: Fire, Poison

Damage Resistances: Non-magical physical

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: Auran, Common

Challenge: 7 (2,900 XP)

Passive

Blazing Weapons

Any landed attack sets that 5 foot square ablaze (1d8 per turn). The fire lasts for 1 minute unless extinguished.

Actions

Multiattack: The aspect of Fire makes two greataxe attacks.

Sweltering Greataxe: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: (2d10 + 4) slashing/fire damage

Immolate (Recharge 5 or 6). The aspect targets a 15 foot cone. Each target in the cone must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, a target takes (4d8 + 4) fire damage (half on a save) and the ground in that area is set on fire.
