Chitterer Brood Queen

Deep within the chittering dark lies the brood queen of the chitterers, a massive bloated insect brood queen responsible for birthing all of the chitterers that plague the cave systems. The arcane energy emitted by the incident has seeped into the hive's lifeblood and has made the brood mother that much more deadly.

Stats

Race: Chitterer (Large Monstrosity)

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 13 (Natural armor)

Hit Points: 93

Speed: 15 ft, burrow 10 ft.

STR: 18 (+4) | DEX: 10 (+0) | CON: 17 (+3) | INT: 3 (-4) | WIS: 10 (+0) | CHA: 4 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: Force, Acid

Damage Vulnerabilities: Lightning, Fire

Skills: None

Senses: Darkvision 60 ft, tremorsense 60 ft, passive Perception 10

Languages: N/A

Challenge: 5 (1800 XP)

Passive

Unstable Body: Melee attacks will cause unstable arcane energy to burst out dealing 1d2 damage to the attacker.

Brood: Every turn, roll a D4, the brood queen lays D4 - 1 broodlings (see spider in monster manual).

Actions

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage

Acid Spit: Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 30 ft, 1 target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) acid damage
