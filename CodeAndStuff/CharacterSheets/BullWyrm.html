Collapsin' Bull Wyrm

Big, scary, and pink. The collapsin' bull wyrm was named for the bull-like horns it uses to sense movement as well as its tendency to collapse manmade tunnels while boring underground. Like their airborne relatives, the bull wyrm is known for hoarding valuable minerals, but displays less intelligence and an even more violent temperament. They are not carnivorous, instead getting their nutrients by absorbing it through the earth, but their gyrating teeth and razor sharp horns make them quite formidable when defending their territory.

"The easiest way to listen for the collapsin' bull wyrm is by putting your ear to the ground and listening for panicked screaming from the miners." -Urgaz Ironflint, Dwarven Naturalist
