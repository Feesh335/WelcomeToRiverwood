Angler

Anglers are not actually chitterers, but an insect that has formed a mutual symbiosis and now resides in their nests. Anglers are sedentary insects that stick to the ceiling and pick off those trying to run away from the swarm with their nematocyst lined grabbing tongue, which paralyzes prey. Odds and ends that the angler can not consume fall below which the chitterers carry off to their kill rooms.

Stats

Race: Medium Beast

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 16 (natural armor)

Hit Points: 28

Speed: 0 ft

STR: 14 (+2) | DEX: 1 (-5) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 4 (-3) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 4 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 12

Languages: None

Challenge: 5 (1,800 XP)

Passive

Disguised

The angler is difficult to tell from the rocks on the cave ceiling and has advantage on rolls to stay hidden.

Actions

Drag: Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 30 ft., one creature. Hit (1d4) bludgeoning damage plus (2d6) poison damage and the target must succeed on a DC 16 constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute and be grappled and restrained to the angler (escape DC 14). While poisoned this way, the target is paralyzed. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Munch: Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit (2d6 +2) slashing damage.
