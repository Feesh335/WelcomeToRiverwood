Broodmother

The queen of a chitterer colony is a degree of magnitude more ferocious, intelligent, and important to the colony's survival than any other subtype of chitterer. In the event of a broodmother dying, the colony is thrown into disarray and begins showing all manner of erratic behavior until a larvaling mutates into a new broodmother. If this fails to happen, the colony will collapse entirely. The broodmother is fed a peculiar "soup" of volcanic fungus rich in mithril, which reinforces the outer chitin layer and making it exceptionally difficult to pierce. Only the colony's praetors are allowed to carry this secret mixture off to the queen. Although few subterranean creatures pose a threat to the broodmother, the colony goes to great efforts to keep her hidden and digs deep underground "royal chambers" which only the praetors know the location of. This particular broodmother has begun to become warped by the taint of the arcane energy in the food brought to her by the hive and is growing exceptionally large and overloading with unstable energy.

Stats

Race: Huge Beast

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 21 (mithril infused natural armor)

Hit Points: 333 (50 points for shell)

Speed: 25 ft, 10ft burrow

STR: 24 (+7) | DEX: 6 (-2) | CON: 22 (+6) | INT: 6 (-2) | WIS: 12 (+1) | CHA: 8 (-1)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: Force

Damage Resistances: Non-mithril melee damage, fire

Damage Vulnerabilities: All but force when shell crust is destroyed

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 12

Languages: None

Challenge: 17 (18,000 XP)

Passive

Encrusted Shell

The abdomen on the broodmother's back is coated with a layer of metallic mineral slag. If destroyed, the abdomen becomes vulnerable to all types of damage. Once destroyed, this shell regenerates at the beginning of the next round.

Unstable Blood

Whenever damaged while the shell is destroyed, pulses of unstable energy radiate out from the broodmother dealing 1d10 force damage to all targets within 5 feet.

Lair Actions

At the beginning of a round, chitterers will answer the broodmother's call. Roll a D20 to determine what arrives. 1-2: 1 Praetor 3-7: 3 Chitterwings 8-11: 3 Chitdozers 12-16: 4 Swarmers 17-19: 4 Chitterers 20: 2 Glimmerhides

Legendary Actions

Bite (1): Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft, one creature. Hit: (2d12 + 8) piercing damage and the target is grappled (Escape DC 16)

Tainted Slagball (2): Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 50 feet, one creature. Hit (6d10 + 5) fire damage and an additional 2d10 force damage to all targets within 10 feet.

Actions

Multiattack: The broodmother makes three attacks, one with its bite, and two with its claws.

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft, one creature. Hit: (2d12 + 8) piercing damage and the target is grappled (Escape DC 16).

Claw: Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft, one creature. Hit: (2d10 + 6) slashing damage.

Spike Eruption (Recharge 5-6): Spikes of jagged stone erupt all around the broodmother. Each creature within 15 feet of the broodmother must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes (7d10) piercing damage, or half as much on a successful save.
