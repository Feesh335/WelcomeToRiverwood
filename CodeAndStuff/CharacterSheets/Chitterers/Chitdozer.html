Chitdozer

Acting as a sort of cavalry for the drones, chitdozers are the first into the fray when the swarm chooses to attack something. Chitdozers have hardened chitin shells that makes them very difficulty to damage when curled up. While curled up, they also use their acid launching pustules to boost themselves along, giving them remarkable speeds to ram into things and charge into the fray. They are much more vulnerable when unfurled, but can launch pinpoint accurate acid nodes towards targets after breaking the line.

Passive

Rollin'

If the chitdozer moves at least 15 feet straight while rolling, it has advantage on its next melee attack.

Actions

Acid Lob: Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 30/90ft. Hit (2d10 + 2) acid damage.
