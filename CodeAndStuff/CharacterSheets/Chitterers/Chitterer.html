Chitterer

A common drone that completely infests the cave systems in the chittering dark and lends it the "chittering" part of the name. Chitterers feed on decaying matter and are known for swarming and dismembering their prey before bringing the parts to a "kill room" where the parts ferment near thermal vents which attracts symbiotic fungus for eating. The overwhelming majority of chitterers are drones which use swarm tactics to overwhelm prey before retrieving the remnants and storing them in the base. Only a brood queen is capable of breeding new chitterers, and so the colony will protect the queen at all costs, usually keeping her hidden away in secret underground chambers with only "royal" subsets of chitterers called "Praetors" knowing the queen's location. Some chitterers will take on specialized evolutions to assist the hive with opponents that the drones struggle to deal with, while others evolve to fill some other niche within the hive, often becoming deadlier in the process. Although unintelligent, chitterers are just smart enough to know that tasks are easier to perform in greater numbers and will prefer to alert nearby drones when they feel outnumbered. A single chitterer is easily dealt with, but a single chitterer is either a hypothetical, or the tip of a chitinous iceberg, and many of the more specialized chitterers can strike fear into even the most seasoned of adventurers. For a mining colony to be set up so close to a chitterer hive walks the line between foolish and suicidal.
