Chitterwing

Some drones are specialized for flight and will scout out areas outside of the hive looking for sources of food. Once located, they leave a pheromone letting the hive know the location and begin dropping prey from the sky to the waiting swarms, letting gravity do most of the work. Once a raid is completed, they will expedite the process of carrying back the food sources by flying them to the entrance. Chitterwings will occasionally appear in larger underground chambers in which moving prey up and down would be too time consuming otherwise. When attacked, they prefer to drag targets into the air with them and drop them far below to the awaiting swarms.
