Furnacewalker

These exceptionally large specialized chitterers have evolved to serve a dual role as a sort of chef and defender that spends its entire life within the chitterer's kill rooms. They regulate temperatures by taking magma into thier bodies and heating or cooling it using chemical processes with the thermal regulation glands on its back to provide ideal growing temperatures for the various types of volcanic fungus grown within the room. If the kill room is threatened, the furnacewalker can also expel the magma forcefully, creating difficult terrain for the would be attackers. Its exceptional size also allows it bring parts of the cave down if needed. When critically injured, the thermal regulation glands may go critical and begin building levels of heat that the furnacewalker can not sustain, causing a massive explosion likely to destroy the entire room. Exercise extreme caution around injured furnacewalkers. The loss of a kill room is disruptive to a chitterer colony, but recoverable. In the event that one is destroyed, a larva will begin mutating into a furnacewalker again.

Stats

Race: Huge Beast

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 17 (natural armor)

Hit Points: 240

Speed: 20 ft

STR: 26 (+8) | DEX: 8 (-1) | CON: 20 (+5) | INT: 4 (-3) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 4 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: Fire

Damage Resistances: Acid, Poison, non-magic physical

Damage Vulnerabilities: Frost

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: None

Challenge: 13 (100 XP)

Passive

Unstable

While below 25% health or when killed, roll a D20, on a 7 or lower the furnacewalker goes critical and explodes, dying instantly and dealing 20d10 fire damage to all targets within 30 feet. This also affects the room that the furnacewalker is in, likely destroying parts of the terrain. This effect does not occur if defeated with frost damage or if the furnacewalker is frozen.

Actions

Multiattack: The furnacewalker does one slam and one other attack.

Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 24 (4d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

Expel Magma (recharge 5,6): The furnacewalker exhales magma in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking (12d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. This area remains ablaze for one minute, dealing 1d6 fire damage per tile entered for the next minute.

Erupt: Launches a 5th level fireball with save DC 17 at a target within 60 feet. (10d6 fire)
