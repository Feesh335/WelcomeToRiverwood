Glimmerhide

Inedible to chitterers, and highly desired by miners due to the precious minerals that build up in their guts, the glimmerhide has formed a symbiotic relationship with the chitterers, who keep their would be predators at bay while they slowly feed on detritus deep in the soil. Considerable gold and gem deposits can be found inside if killed before they can flee.
