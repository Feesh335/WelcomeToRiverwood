Praetor

Praetors are an "upper caste" of the chitterers and serve as both brood guards and the only members of the hive allowed to be in contact with the broodmother to whom they dig out her nests, feed her, and guard her spawn. When the drones begin giving off a distress signal, praetors will answer the call and use their physical might to do away with whatever might threaten the hive. Their mandible guards take in some of the mithril from carrying the broodmother's "soup" and are nearly unbreakable as a result. The backs of their bodies do not share this trait and are much more easily damaged. The praetors will remedy this by trying to choose narrow passageways attack in, or burrowing in a manner where only their head sticks out.

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Languages: None

Challenge: 9 (5000 XP)

Passive

Royalguard

When a creature within 5 feet of you makes an attack against a target other than you (and that target doesn't have this feat), you can use your reaction to make a melee weapon attack against the attacking creature. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from you even if they take the Disengage action before leaving your reach. When you hit a creature with an opportunity attack, the creature's speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn.

Actions

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft, one creature. Hit: (3d10 + 6) piercing damage

Acid Spray(Recharge 6): The praetor exhales a 15-foot cone of boiling acid. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking (8d8) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
