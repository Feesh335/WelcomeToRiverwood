Chitterer Swarm

Only a fraction of these larval chitterers will reach adulthood, but thier instinct to swarm is much stronger in these early stages. Swarms of larval chitterers still have no issue with shredding much larger beasts down to size with their small but razor sharp mandibles.

Stats

Race: Tiny Beast

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 12 (natural armor)

Hit Points: 35

Speed: 35 ft, burrow 10ft

STR: 6 (-2) | DEX: 8 (-1) | CON: 8 (-1) | INT: 4 (-3) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 4 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: None

Challenge: 2 (100 XP)

Passive

Swarm

The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny insect. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. While above half health, the swarm has a bonus to hit, damage, and advantage on its attacks.

Actions

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 (swarm) + 2 to hit, reach 5 ft, one creature. Hit: (swarm - 5d4 +5) (2d4 + 2) piercing damage
