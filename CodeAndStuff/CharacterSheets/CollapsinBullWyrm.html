Collapsin' Bull Wyrm

Big, scary, and pink. The collapsin' bull wyrm was named for the bull-like horns it uses to sense movement as well as its tendency to collapse manmade tunnels while boring underground. Like their airborne relatives, the bull wyrm is known for hoarding valuable minerals, but displays less intelligence and an even more violent temperament. They are not carnivorous, instead getting their nutrients by absorbing it through the earth, but their gyrating teeth and razor sharp horns make them quite formidable when defending their territory.

"The easiest way to listen for the collapsin' bull wyrm is by putting your ear to the ground and listening for panicked screaming from the miners." -Urgaz Ironflint, Dwarven Naturalist

Stats

Race: Bore Wyrm

Alignment: Gargantuan Beast, Unaligned

Armor Class: 20 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 233 (Diceroll)

Speed: 50 feet, burrow 30ft (movement type)

STR: 26 (+8) | DEX: 8 (-1) | CON: 24 (+7) | INT: 3 (-4)| WIS: 8 (-1)| CHA: 4 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Con + 11, Str + 4, Wis + 4

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Fire, non-magical physical damage

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: None

Senses: Blindsight 50 feet, tremorsense 60 feet, passive perception 9

Languages: None

Challenge: 14 (11,500)

Passive

Tunneler. The wyrm can burrow through solid rock at half its burrow speed and leaves a 10 foot diameter tunnel in its wake.

Actions

Multiattack: The wyrm makes one attack with its jaws/breath and one with its stinger.

Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 feet, one target. Hit: (3d8+9) slashing damage.

Stinger: Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft,. one creature. Hit (3d6 + 9) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 18 constitution saving throw, taking (12d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Knife Breath (recharges 6): The wyrm exhales a flurry of sharpened metals from its stomach in a 30 foot cone in front of it. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes (10d8) slashing damage, or half as much on a successful one.
