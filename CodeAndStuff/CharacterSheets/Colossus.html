Colossus of Mundus

A 14 foot tall colossus of mud and stones, given life by the will of Mundus. Slow and towering, these colossi lord over the battlefield, crushing the slow and misfortunate who stand in its way. The Mundus Faithful will invoke a colossus of Mundus to cut through the enemy lines and any fortifications with ease. Lacks the mobility and resistances associated with earth elementals and makes up for it with raw might.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: None

Armor Class: 17 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 115

Speed: 15 ft. (Walk, Burrow)

STR: 23 (+6) | DEX: 4 (-3) | CON: 20 (+5) | INT: 2 (-4) | WIS: 8 (-1) | CHA: 5 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Slashing

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Terran, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 5 (1,800 XP)

Passive

Blessing of Mundus: Targets within one tile of the colossus (including the colossus) have advantage and +2 AC against spell damage.

Siege Monster: The colossus deals double damage to objects and structures.

Fury of Mundus: If damaged by a spell on the previous turn, the colossus can use any of its actions as a bonus action.

Actions

Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft, 1 target. Hit: (2d12+6) bludgeoning damage

Jagged Eruption: Range: Self (25 foot cone) Duration: Instantaneous, Effect: The earth ruptures and impales those in a cone before the colossus. Each creature in a 25-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 4d8+3 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Bury: The colossus buries target of size class medium or smaller grappled by it. The buried target is blinded, restrained, and unable to breathe, and it must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw at the start of each of the colossus's turns or take 2d8+4 bludgeoning damage.
