Corlith

The inseperable twin brother of Anilor, Corlith only inherited partial divine blood, allowing him to hear the voices of his ancestors. Deprived of the full power of the divine blood, he instead acquired power through tireless training in the field of battle, becoming an accomplished warrior fighting for the causes identified by Anilor. When Anilor received a vision of a series of disasters stemming from the Mollinstead region, Corlith was the first to respond, ready to lay down his life for the sake of his brother. When Anilor received visions of a black seed falling from the sky, Corlith decided to safeguard Anilor but fell to the influence of Krimerus within the meteor, becoming his avatar within Mollinstead. With his body and mind hollowed out, he has taken on a new identity as the prince of worms.

Abilities

Damage Immunities: All Spell Damage

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Languages: Common, Abyssal

Challenge: 21 (33,000 XP)

Passive

Body of the Worm

Corlith is immune to all spell damage.

Return to Worms

When killed, Corlith becomes a swarm of worms that will reform one day later unless destroyed.

Spirit Shields

When Anilor takes damage and Corlith is within 30 feet, he can reduce the damage taken by 4d6 and deal that much damage to the attacker as a reaction.

Actions

Multiattack: Corlith attacks twice with his pike.

Consumed Pike: Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. Hit: (2d10 + 5 piercing damage, 2d10 psychic damage).

Whirlwind: Strikes all targets within range of Corlith.
