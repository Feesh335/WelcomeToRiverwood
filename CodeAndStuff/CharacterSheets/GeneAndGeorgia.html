Governor Gene and Lady Georgia

The ever devoted governor of Vellis and his lovely wife who have stayed behind to take care of their fair citizens. Or, at least that's what they want for you to believe. In reality, Lady Georgia is a green hag who has come across an amulet from the Collector and has been convinced to sacrifice citizens to her amulet as offerings while Governor Gene is a mutated cult fanatic with an obsession for the arcane. If the party tries to investigate the basement (where the hostages are) and fails the perception check before that, they will drop the illusion and ambush the players. Gene is using an illusory appearance from Georgia to keep up appearances but his true form is a warped arcane mutant with hooked limbs that allow him to run up walls and hang from the ceiling to ambush.

"Ah, Gene, look, a group of adventurers has managed to bust down the barricade they sealed us in our home with, we're free now! Thank you for saving us!"
