Golem of Mundus

A 7 foot tall amalgam of stones, given life by the will of Mundus. Strong, tough, and able to be summoned with a reasonable offering to Mundus, these golems are often seen accompanying the Mundus Faithful when they set out to fight. Their resistance to magic and ability to hurl chunks of rock from a distance have spelled out the end for many who have opposed the will of Mundus.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: None

Armor Class: 17 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 70

Speed: 20 ft. (Walk, Burrow)

STR: 19 (+4) | DEX: 4 (-3) | CON: 19 (+4) | INT: 2 (-4) | WIS: 8 (-1) | CHA: 5 (-3)

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Slashing

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Terran, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 3 (700 XP)

Passive

Blessing of Mundus: Targets within one tile of the golem (including the golem) have advantage against spell damage.

Actions

Crush: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: (2d10+3) bludgeoning damage

Rock Blast: Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 30/90 ft., one target. Hit: (1d10+2) piercing damage.
