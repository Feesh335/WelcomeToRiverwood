Manos, Envoy of the Nightmare

A hand from the realm of nightmares capable of shaping the shadows into creatures resembling someone's fears. The eye in the palm sees everything that causes grief in the victim so that Manos can shape the shadows into their punishment. Normally carrying out the will of the Nightmare Court's ruling class, it has been summoned forth by Meridan as the court owes him a favor for enabling their passage into Mollinstead.

Stats

Race: Aberration

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Armor Class: 18 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 160

Speed: 40 ft. (Flight)

STR: 24 (+7) | DEX: 14 (+2) | CON: 18 (+4) | INT: 12 (+1) | WIS: 12 (+1) | CHA: 10 (-1)

Saving Throws: Str +9, Con +7, Wis +3, Cha +3

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Necrotic, Psychic

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: Radiant

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Deepspeech, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 8

Passive

None

Actions

Gaze: Range 60 ft, single target. Manos gazes into the target's soul and causes the next shape shadows to resemble the target's fears

Shape Shadows: Sculpts a glob of shadows into a living creature hostile to the target.

Crush: Attempts to grapple the target, if succsesful, crushes the target for 4d10 psychic damage. The target must then succeed on a DC 13 wisdom saving throw or drop whatever they're holding and become fearful of Manos.
