Father Narlan

Arch-priest of the Mundus Faithful in Southern Mollinstead. A man of faith who founded the namesake priory next to the temple of Mundus. His priory acts as both a place of training for priests of Mundus as well as an orphanage that raises children in the way of the faith. He was quick to realize Alanna's passion for the Mundus faith and helped her to hone her abilities as a priestess. Claims to be just another arch-priest in a long line of arch-priests, but in reality he is a prophet of Mundus who is silently carrying out the will of Mundus for the day that he returns. He will do whatever it takes to defend the church and the priory.

"Do you feel it heroes? A rumble in the earth, and a change in the winds of fate. There are powers in play here far beyond our little priory, it would be wise to remember that."
