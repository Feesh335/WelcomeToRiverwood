Ogmund the Bold

An elite bounty hunter who leans on his tech and minions to get the job done. A leading member of the Golden Circle who lives for the drama of a good fight even if gets in the way of his contract.

Stats

Race: Monari

Alignment: (Neutral Evil)

Armor Class: 19 (Battle Composite)

Hit Points: 300

Speed: 45 ft (run)

STR: 22 (+6) | DEX: 24 (+7) | CON: 24 (+7) | INT: 18 (+4) | WIS: 24 (+7) | CHA: 20 (+5)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Con + 10, Int + 10, Wis + 10, Dex +14

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: Non-magical physical attacks

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: Arcana +18, History +18, Insight +14, Religion +18

Senses: Truesight 90 Feet, passive Perception 15

Languages: Common, Abyssal, Orcish

Challenge: 20 (25,000 XP)

Passive

Burst-jet Augmentation: As a reaction, Ogmund can activate a burst-jet to grant himself +7 AC, move 15 feet without provoking opportunity attacks, and dealing 1d6 fire damage to all targets within 5 feet of him.

Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Ogmund fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Chosen Quarry: If Ogmund use Hunter's Mark on a target, all allies gain the benefit against that target as well.

Legendary Actions

Ogmund can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Ogmund regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

Oil Trap (costs 1 action): Plants an oil trap on the ground within 5 feet of Ogmund. When stepped on, splashes oil on all targets within a 15 foot radius who fail a DC 19 Dex save. Oil covered targets have their movement halved and become flammable. An ignited target takes 1d12 damage per turn until extinguished.

Burst-jets (costs 1 action): Ogmund activates his burst jets, moving him 15 feet without provoking opportunity attacks and burning all targets within 5 feet for 1d6 fire damage.

Grenade Trap (costs 2 actions): Ogmund hides an acid grenade on the ground within 5 feet of him. Activates an acid fireball if activated.

Sleeping Gas (costs 2 actions): Ogmund activates a can of sleep gas around him. All targets within 20 feet of him must pass a DC 19 constitution saving throw or be put to sleep. This effects last for 1 minute, or until the target is damaged or awoken by another target.

Crawlers (costs 3 actions): Ogmund lets out (1d6+2) crawler mines from his bag around him. Crawlers will seek out a target and explode for 4d6 fire damage, halved if the target succeeds on a DC19 dex saving throw. Crawlers have 14 AC, 12 HP and 25 movement.

Actions

Multiattack: Ogmund can make either 2 melee attacks and 1 ranged attack.

The Spinetingler: Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 10 ft, 1 target(s). Hit: (4d8 +6) (piercing) damage and (2d8 + 2) electrical damage. Target must succeed on a DC 19 constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of next turn.

The Headcannon: Ranged Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 90 ft, 1 target(s). Hit: 8d8 + 8 thunder damage.

Spellcasting. Ranger spells. Yeah.
