Runic Abomination

This horrifying amalgamation is the result of a bag of Bound runestones trying to bind the same few souls, warping them beyond recognition in the process. The resulting abomination is no longer capable of thought beyond its base instincts and lashes out at everything in a fit of psychic agony. Its form shifts about constantly, but it remains roughly 10 feet tall and strikes out with its warped tentacle like limbs.

"Ah, you must be talking about that... Thing. I'm a trained soldier, and staving off my fears to stay in the fight is second nature, but no amount of training could have prepared me for... That. The way it slinks around with the grace of a dancer despite having the form of an ogre, or the way it calmly stays in the corner murmuring before it suddenly begins wailing and thrashing about, it all just sets my nerves ablaze. But I could just ignore all of it, chalking it up as some unknowable horror that emerged from one of the artifacts after the blast, if it weren't for the way it seems to sound like my old friend Wilhelm who was on guard duty down there during the blast. We all like to try to forget about it. If you get close to the stairs leading to the vault you can hear its warped screaming in your head, and so we all give it wide berth. Whatever business you have down there, I will have no part in it. For that matter, forget that I ever brought it up. If I were still human, I don't know that I'd ever be able to sleep again." -Footman Gorloch

Psychic Wail:

Stats

Race: Bound (Abomination)

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 15 (Mage Armor)

Hit Points: 146

Speed: 30ft

STR: 18 (+4) | DEX: 15 (+2) | CON: 20 (+5) | INT: 4 (-3)| WIS: 6 (-2) | CHA: 6 (-2)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Con + 6, Str + 5

Damage Immunities: Poison, Force

Damage Resistances: None

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Ability X Feet, passive Perception #

Languages: None (Randomly whispers Common via telepathy, 60 ft)

Challenge: 7 (2,900 XP)

Passive

Bound: Healed by exposure to arcane energy, all stats drop by 2 while under half health, becomes dormant upon reaching 0 health if runestone is not destroyed.

Maddening Whispers: Whispers to a random target at the beginning of its turn. The target must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened on their next turn.

Shifting Form: At the start of the abomination's turn, roll a D20, on an 11 or higher, it enters destroyer form, otherwise it enters stalker form.

Actions

Forms: While in destroyer form, the abomination will pick a nearby target and use a single slam. While in stalker form, the abomination will pick a further away target and use two impaling strikes.

Runic Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: (4d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Impaling Strike: Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5ft, 1 target. Hit: (2d10 + 2) piercing damage.

Psychic Wail: Once per combat, the abomination can unleash a psychic wail. Each creature within 60 ft must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or take (2d6 + 3) psychic damage and be feared for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
