Servant of Mundus

A 4.5 foot tall lump of earth, given life by the will of Mundus. Although lacking sentience, the servant of Mundus is notably more intelligent than the other variants that can be summoned. As such, servants of Mundus are often summoned for more civil tasks such as construction and farming while the other variants are summoned near exclusively for combat. Servants of Mundus are also quite strong for their size and still serve a role in defending the Mundus Faithful in combat although the stronger variants are preferred when available.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: None

Armor Class: 13 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 18

Speed: 20 ft. (Walk, Burrow)

STR: 14 (+2) | DEX: 6 (-2) | CON: 11 (+0) | INT: 6 (-2) | WIS: 8 (-1) | CHA: 6 (-2)

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Slashing

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Terran, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 1/4 (50 XP)

Passive

Blessing of Mundus: Targets within one tile of the servant (including the servant) have +3 AC against spell damage.

Actions

Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: (1d6+1) bludgeoning damage

Earthshaping: Creates a small mound of earth acting as cover on one tile. Lasts for 30 seconds.
