Servants of Mundus

As the guardian of the Earthen Realm, it's not entirely surprising that the Earth itself will rise in the name of Mundus to defend itself. Servants of Mundus bear many similarities to Earth elementals, but differ in that they are temporary and form from whatever type of ground surrounds the summoner performing the ritual of Mundus. The size and power of the servant will vary based on both the experience of the summoner as well as the power of the artifact being cleansed, with novice summoners with weak artifacts summoning little more than charmed pebbles while large scale rituals with powerful artifacts can raise the very hills they are performed on. The servants all share a very basic intelligence, only being able to follow the simplest of orders and will often ignore said orders if they see an opportunity to attack what they perceive as being otherworldly magic (i.e. not nature based). The duration of a servant will vary based on the summoner and artifact, but generally last for a few hours before the will of Mundus fades from it and it returns to being a normal chunk of Earth.
