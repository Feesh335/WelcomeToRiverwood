Shardling of Mundus

A fist sized geode, given life by the will of Mundus. Although largely incapable of fighting on its own, it can be used as an improvised projectile and seems to enjoy being tossed around. Also gives off a small forcefield that dampens the effects of spells. It is a matter of some debate among the Mundus Faithful as to whether or not Mundus helps to guide the Shardling when thrown, or if it's just easy to toss because of its shape.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: None

Armor Class: 14 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 9

Speed: 10 ft. (Walk, Burrow)

STR: 8 (-1) | DEX: 3 (-4) | CON: 11 (+0) | INT: 1 (-5) | WIS: 8 (-1) | CHA: 6 (-2)

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Slashing

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Terran, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 0 (10 XP)

Passive

Blessing of Mundus: Targets within one tile of the shardling have +2 AC against spell damage.

Stone Toss: The shardling can be used as an improvised throwing weapon and will try to guide itself towards the target. Light, Ranged, Thrown (Strength), (20/60), +5 to hit, deals 1d4 + 1 bludgeoning damage

Carry: A creature of size class small or greater can carry the shardling. While carrying the shardling, that creature has the blessing of Mundus effect.

Actions

Tackle: Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft, 1 target. Hit: 2 (1d2 + 1) bludgeoning damage
