Slug, the Bandit Captain

A rough and tough bandit captain enjoying the lack of order that followed the arcane incident. Nicknamed for his infamous love of bareknuckle brawling, if Slug can't eat it, fight it, or fuck it, he's not interested.

"What? Barkeep got you looking for this? *holds up barrel of hops* You're dumber than I am if you think I'm giving it up for free, but show me a good time and we'll talk.

Actions

Multiattack: Slug makes three melee attacks: two with his scimitar and one with his fist.

Schimitar: Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage.

Right Hook: A fierce right hook: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or take 8 (2d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage and be stunned until the end of Slug's next turn. On a successful save, the target takes half the bludgeoning damage and isn't stunned.
