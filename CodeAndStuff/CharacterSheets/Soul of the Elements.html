Soul of the Elements

A soul imbued with energy from the four elements. Highly unstable.

Stats

Race: Elemental

Alignment: Unaligned

Armor Class: 16 (arcane barrier)

Hit Points: 200

Speed: 30 ft (hover)

STR: 18 (+4) | DEX: 18 (+4) | CON: 18 (+4) | INT: 10 (0) | WIS: 10 (0) | CHA: 10 (0)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: None

Damage Immunities: Thunder, Lightning, Frost, Fire, Poison

Damage Resistances: Non-magical physical

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Senses: Darkvision, 60 feet, passive Perception 10

Languages: Auran, Common

Challenge: 8 (3,900 XP)

Passive

Unstable weaponry

Selects one of the aspects weapons at random per hit

Actions

Multiattack: The aspect of air makes two weapon attacks and a surge.

Unstable weapon: Roll a D4 per attack, select that aspect's weapon attack.

Unstable surge: Roll a D4, select a random aspect's cooldown ability.
