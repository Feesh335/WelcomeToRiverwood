Cervadeus, the Corrupted Guardian

Before the incident, Cervadeus was a massive stag revered by the local druids as a minor deity. Following the incident, Cervadeus has become one of the largest known arc-giants who now reigns supreme over the woods surrounding Meridan's point. With nothing capable of challenging it, Cervadeus now wanders the woods in search of more sources of arcane energy to sustain its unstable form.

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Con + 11, Dex + 7

Damage Immunities: Force

Damage Resistances: Poison

Damage Vulnerabilities: Anti-magic Zone

Condition Immunities: exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained

Senses: Darkvision 120 ft, passive Perception 8

Languages: -

Challenge: 20 (25,000 XP)

Passive

Unstable Aura: Any creature that starts its turn within 30 feet of Cervadeus must succeed on a DC 21 Constitution saving throw or take (4d6) force damage on a failed save or (2d6) force damage on a successful save.

Unstable Essence: Cervadeus regenerates (6d10) hit points per turn while in an area with high concentrations of arcane energy. Cervadeus will also prioritize seeking out arcane artifacts to heal himself with.

Immutable Form: Cervadeus is immune to any spell or effect that would alter his form.

Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Cervadeus fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Siege Monster. Cervadeus deals double damage to objects and structures.

Actions

Panic Attacks: Cervadeus makes three actions only if below half health.

Stomp: Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: (3d12 + 8) bludgeoning damage.

Ram: Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: (4d12 + 10) bludgeoning damage.

Charge: Cervadeus charges at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits a 5 foot radius surrounding the target with his horns, the target takes (8d6) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.

Siphon Energy: Cervadeus siphons the arcane energy stored in a relic, healing himself for (10d10) hit points. Cervadeus can only perform this action if a target within melee range has an arcane relic and can only perform this once per turn.

Legendary Actions

Cervadeus can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Cervadeus regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Quake (Costs 2 Actions): Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 20 ft cone. Hit: (3d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

Forest's Call: All terrain within 30 ft of Cervadeus becomes difficult terrain for 1 minute.
