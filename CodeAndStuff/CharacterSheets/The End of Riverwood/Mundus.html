Aspect of Mundus

Known to many as the Magic Breaker, Mundus is the patron god of the appropriately named Mundus Faithful. Championing the causes of the downtrodden by drawing out their inner strength and teaching them to reject the influences of the outside realms, Mundus is anathema to all things magical, for better or worse. In truly dire situations, a powerful follower of Mundus making a tremendous sacrifice - an arcane relic of unfathomable power or their very life - may allow for Mundus to send aid to the world in the form of a divine aspect, a physical manifestation of his earthly form, carrying his memories, will, and a significant portion of his strength. Eras have sometimes gone by without Mundus sending forth his aspect to carry out his will, but it is always brought forth for the same reason: to turn back the damage inflicted by those who dabble in the arcane, a task that none have succeeded in seeing that it is not carried out.

Stats

Race: Humanoid (Titan)

Alignment: True Neutral

Armor Class: 21 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 600

Speed: 50 ft

STR: 30 (+10) | DEX: 28 (+9) | CON: 28 (+9) | INT: 20 (+5)| WIS: 24 (+7) | CHA: 22 (+6)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Dex +11, Con +16, Wis +13, Cha +12

Skills: Initimidation +20, Perception+20

Damage Immunities: All magical attacks

Damage Resistances: Poison

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Condition Immunities: exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained

Senses: truesight 120 ft, passive Perception 27

Languages: -

Challenge: 30 (155,000 XP)

Passive

Immutable Form: The Aspect of Mundus is immune to any spell or effect that would alter his form.

Legendary Resistance (5/Day). If the Aspect of Mundus fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Magic Breaker: On being hit by Mundus, have Mundus and the target roll a D20. Subtract the target's D20 + their proficiency bonus from Mundus's D20 and deplete all spell slots of that level and lower. An antimagic zone with a radius of 10 feet is always present surrounding the Avatar of Mundus.

Divine Presense: Each creature of Mundus's choice within 120 feet of him must succeed on a DC 22 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened for 1 minute. This roll is repeated at the end of each creature's turn until this effect is dispelled.

Actions

Multiattack: Mundus can punch twice and use one Grasp of the Earth.

Punch: Melee Weapon Attack: +18 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (5d12 + 6) bludgeoning damage. Target must succeed on a DC 26 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Purge: Melee Weapon Attack: +18 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Target must succeed on a DC 26 Wisdom saving throw or lose all spell slots and any active magical effects.

Grasp of the Earth: A massive fist of earth forms and slams down on an area with a 15 foot radius. Each creature in that area must make a DC 26 Dexterity saving throw or take 5d12+6 bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.

Legendary Actions

The Aspect of Mundus can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Aspect of Mundus regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Punch (Costs 1 Action): Performs a punch against a target.

Heroic Leap (Costs 1 Action): Leaps to a location within 60 feet. All targets within 10 feet of the landing area must succeed on a DC 26 Dexterity saving throw or take 2d12 bludgeoning damage and have disadvantage on the next attack.

Planet Breaker (Costs 3 Actions): Focuses all power on the next punch, causing it to deal 3X as much damage and destroying a cylinder of ground with a 5 foot radius and 100 foot cylinder centered on the target. All targets within 100 feet must succeed on a DC 26 Constitution saving throw or take 1d12 bludgeoning damage and be deafened until the end of their next turn.
