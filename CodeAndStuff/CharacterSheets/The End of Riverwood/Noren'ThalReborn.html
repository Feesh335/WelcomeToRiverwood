Noren'Thal Reborn

Epstein Noren'Thal didn't kill himself. Well, sort of... He used create Clone and hid the clone in the Collection before using the runeblade to kill himself, transferred his soul to the clone without being Bound anymore, and then used Gorloch's runestone as a powersource/disguise for his suit. This is a different suit built for a humanoid occupant, but made to look the same to avoid arousing any suspicion.

Stats

Race: High Elf (Nere'Vei)

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Armor Class: 20 (Biomechanical Plate)

Hit Points: 162 + remaining overshield

Speed: 30 ft

STR: 12 (+1) | DEX: 16 (+3) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 24 (+7) | WIS: 20 (+5) | CHA: 14 (+2)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws Con +9, Int +13, Wis +10

Damage Immunities: Force

Damage Resistances: Poison, Psychic

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: Arcana +15, History +15, Insight +12, Perception +12

Senses: truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 20

Languages: Common, Elvish, Deepspeak

Challenge: 22 (41,000 XP)

Passive

Defense Matrix: The Canopy of Dawn is equipped with a shielding system which draws power from Bound runestones, creating an overshield with the health extracted.

Mind Shielding: The Canopy of Dawn shields the wearer's mind, preventing their thoughts from being read. It also offers resistance to anti-magic zones, allowing for Bound to enter anti-magic zones without becoming dormant.

Injection System: Allows the wearer to inject brew once per round as a free action.

Autonomous Limbs: The four spider-like limbs act on their own and are capable of wielding weaponry.

Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Noren'Thal fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

Actions

Sparagmos (Recharge 5-6). The device mounted to the Canopy of Dawn's shoulder fires a beam of white hot light in a 120 foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d10 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Multiattack: Noren'Thal can use Sparagmos and then either a spell or a flurry of melee attacks based on what the limbs are holding.

Random Bullshit Go!: Makes a melee attack with the windup lance (+10, 1d12+1d6+7), 7 hammer (2d6+7, hit again on a 7), and ranged attack with APLO-2750 (1d12+7)

Spells: Cantrips (at will): chill touch (see "Actions" below), fire bolt, mage hand, prestidigitation, ray of frost 1st level (4 slots): magic missile, shield, sleep 2nd level (3 slots): blur, detect thoughts, mirror image 3rd level (3 slots): animate dead, counterspell, fireball, fly, lightning bolt 4th level (3 slots): blight, confusion, polymorph 5th level (3 slots): cloudkill, cone of cold, hold monster, scrying 6th level (2 slots): chain lightning, circle of death, create undead, scrying 7th level (2 slots): finger of death, forcecage, prismatic spray 8th level (1 slot): incendiary cloud, maze 9th level (1 slot): power word kill, time stop

Legendary Actions

Noren'Thal can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Noren'Thal regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

Melee (Costs 1 Action): Performs melee weapon attacks against a target in range.

Spell (Costs 1-3 Actions): Casts a spell up to the level of the actions spent.

Runic Empowerment (Costs 2 Actions): Grafts a rune to the ground allowing the next spell cast from that location to use metamagic.
