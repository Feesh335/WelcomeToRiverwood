Champion Steve

How could I not?

Stats

Race: Human

Alignment: Chaotic Evilish

Armor Class: 16 (natural armor)

Hit Points: 100

Speed: 30 ft

STR: 18 (+4) | DEX: 14 (+2) | CON: 16 (+3) | INT: 8 (-1) | WIS: 12 (+1) | CHA: 14 (+2)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: Str + 6, Con + 6

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: All spell damage

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: Advantage on intimidation checks

Senses: passive Perception 11

Languages: Common

Challenge: 7

Passive

Master Grappler: Steve has advantage on grapple checks and can grapple creatures up to two sizes larger.

Pack Tactics: Steve has advantage on attack rolls if he is within 5 feet of an ally.

Mundus Champion's Belt: Steve has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Actions

Multiattack: Steve makes two attacks with his mace.

Mace: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage.

Stone Cold Stunner: Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target grappled by Steve. Hit: 1d10 + 5 bludgeoning damage. Target must succeed on a DC 15 constitution saving throw or be stunned until this grapple ends.
