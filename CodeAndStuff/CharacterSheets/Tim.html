Name

A silver tongued thief with a nose for treasure and a heart of coal. Took off with the priory's emergency relic and is hoping to loot Fort Cainhurst so he can sell the relics to the needy of Riverwood to make a fortune.

"Look, I'm going to save us both some time and just say this - you don't like me, and I don't like you. The only people tough enough to make it this far north and live to talk about it are unrepenant assholes who aren't afraid to gamble with their lives and unrepenant assholes who aren't afraid to gamble with their lives but have the kind of charisma it takes to convince you that that isn't an accurate description of them. I'm the former, and you look to me like the latter. But let's set that aside for a moment to talk about a certain missing and hopefully dead archmage who fits that second category who stole my wedding ring to "study the arcane properties within" before locking it up in the vault under this keep. Now I'm hardly the sentimental type, but I'll be damned if I'm letting that mage have the last laugh at my expense and walk out of this situation with blue balls and empty pockets. So here's the deal, there's some sort of god forsaken nightmare behind those doors as well as a small mountain of valuables next to it. I can't take that thing alone, and I don't want to find out the hard way that I can't just snatch the treasure and run, so we're going to go in there together, kill that thing, and I'll even let you keep your fair share of the loot, capiche? You can call me Tim. People usually call me Tim the Scoundrel, but there haven't been enough people here for me to wind up with that nickname again, at least not yet.

Stats

Race: Human

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Armor Class: 15 (Studded Leather)

Hit Points: 54

Speed: 30ft

STR: 11 (+0) | DEX: 16 (+3) | CON: 14 (+2) | INT: 13 (+1) | WIS: 11 (+0) | CHA: 16 (+3)

Abilities

Background

Saving Throws: DEX +6, CHA +4

Damage Immunities: None

Damage Resistances: Poison

Damage Vulnerabilities: None

Skills: Acrobatics +6, Deception +5, Perception +3, Stealth +6

Senses: Passive Perception 13

Languages: Thieves' cant, Common, Elvish

Challenge: 7 (2900XP)

Passive

Assassinate: During his first turn, Tim has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn. Any hit Tim scores against a surprised creature is a critical hit.

Evasion: If Tim is subjected to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, Tim instead takes no damage if he succeeds on the saving throw and only half damage if he fails.

Feign Death: If Tim's health drops below 15, he will feign death. While feigning death, he can use a smoke bomb to aid in his getaway, but will accidentally drop the priory relic in the process.

Actions

Multiattack: Tim makes two quick strikes with his daggers.

Poison Dagger: Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
