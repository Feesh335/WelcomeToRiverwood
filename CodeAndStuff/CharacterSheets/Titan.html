Manos, Envoy of the Nightmare

A 36 foot tall titan born of a piece of mountain, given life by the will of Mundus. A true sight to behold, the titan of Mundus is a massive avatar of all that the Mundus faithful hold dear. The resources required to invoke a titan of Mundus are incredible, and as such the situations in which a titan of Mundus is invoked are often the stuff of legends. It's not hard to imagine the wake of destruction left by Mundus when watching an angered titan fighting unrestrained.

Stats

Race: Gargantuan Elemental

Alignment: None

Armor Class: 18 (Natural Armor)

Hit Points: 222

Speed: 20 ft. (Walk, Burrow)

STR: 28 (+9) | DEX: 4 (-3) | CON: 25 (+7) | INT: 2 (-4) | WIS: 8 (-1) | CHA: 9 (-1)

Saving Throws: Str +9, Con +7, Wis +3, Cha +3

Abilities

Background

Damage Immunities: Poison

Damage Resistances: Slashing, All Magical Damage

Damage Vulnerabilities: Thunder

Senses: Darkvision 60 Ft., Tremorsense 60 Ft., passive Perception 10

Languages: Terran, language of its creator (cannot speak)

Challenge: 12 (8,400 XP)

Passive

Blessing of Mundus: Targets within 5 tiles of the titan (including the titan) have advantage and +3 AC against spell damage.

Siege Monster: The titan deals double damage to objects and structures.

Fury of Mundus: If damaged by a spell on the previous turn, the colossus can use any of its actions as a bonus action.

Actions

Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft, 1 target. Hit: (5d8+10) bludgeoning damage

Consuming Earth: Range: Self (50 foot cone) Duration: Instantaneous, Effect: The earth rumbles and consumes those in a cone before the colossus. Each creature in a 50-foot cone must make a Strength saving throw. A creature takes 6d8+3 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. If target creature does not succeed, they become buried.

Bury: The titan buries target of size class large or smaller. The buried target is blinded, restrained, and unable to breathe, and it must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw at the start of each of the titan's turns or take 3d8+4 bludgeoning damage.
